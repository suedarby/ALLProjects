Welcome to the Sewing Project Manager.

This will lead you through the mini tour of the application in it's preview form. This would normally be where you would land upon login.

To navigate this little preview you will have to use the back button or click Welcome on the top navigation as the pages are not interconnected at this time.

The below are a list of links to sample pages with sample data. It is too soon to release a demo version with a database attached so a simple HTML static version will have to do for a sneak peek at what I am working on. The upside to this is that I'll also include some notes on how the page will work once it is live.

Main Dashboard will allow users to add, view, update and delete (of course) various supplies such as Fabric, Notions, Patterns, Tools, and Trims. These sections will allow users to bring the data into a Project which will help track what projects are in the process and eventually what supplies are missing and need to be purchased to start or complete the project.

Projects will not only let you keep a tab on the UFOs in your stash it will eventually add missing items for a project to a shopping list.

Custom Dashboard (still naming things) will again allow users to add, view, update and delete their own Brands (patterns), Categories, Colors (fabric & trims), Images (patterns), Locations (yes, you can call a location "blue tote" or "tool box"!), Sizes (patterns), Status, Styles (patterns), and Tags (yes, interfacing can be assigned to more than one project!) to match your setup.

Future iterations of this app will include tracking quantities of items and adding or subtracting amounts as they are assigned to a project (only used 1/4 yd of trim and have 2.75 yds left? Yes, the system will tell you what you have left so you don't run out!) Eventually I plan to add a variety of reports to show how are tagged the same or how much of a supply you use in a time frame i.e. a year
